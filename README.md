# HR-Analytics-Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an **HR Analytics Dashboard** designed to analyze workforce data, employee distribution, and satisfaction levels within an organization.

The dashboard provides actionable insights to support **HR decision-making**, improve employee engagement, and optimize workforce management.



## Objectives

* Analyze employee demographics (gender, age)
* Track employee satisfaction levels
* Understand job level distribution
* Evaluate business travel patterns
* Identify workforce trends for better HR strategies



## Key Metrics

* **Total Employees:** 1,470
* **Total Male:** 882 (60%)
* **Total Female:** 588 (40%)
* **Average Satisfaction Score:** 2.73 / 5



## Dashboard Insights

### Employee Demographics

* Male employees make up **60%** of the workforce
* Female employees represent **40%**



### Employee Satisfaction

* Average satisfaction score is **2.73**, indicating room for improvement
* Suggests potential issues in engagement or workplace conditions

---

### Business Travel Analysis

* Majority of employees **rarely travel**
* A smaller portion travels frequently or does not travel at all



### Department / Service Status

* Most employees are **actively working**
* A small percentage are **retrenched**, indicating workforce changes

---

### Job Level Distribution

* Highest number of employees are in **Level 1 and Level 2**
* Fewer employees occupy higher job levels (Level 4–5)

---

### Age Distribution

* Balanced distribution between male and female employees
* Helps HR understand workforce structure
